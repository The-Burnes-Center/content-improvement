The show goes on location with creative personalities and productions across the state, and is recognized as one of the premier arts and culture programs on public television. Learn more, watch the latest episodes and view past shows online . Tune in to NJTV on Sundays at 8 PM and Thursday at 11:30 PM. Catch episodes on WHYY every Sunday at 11 AM.
