If you’re looking for export opportunities you’ve come to the right place. The Office of Export Promotion works with New Jersey’s small and mid-sized companies interested in expanding sales and their customer base for your products and services through export. Companies interested in global business activity can tap into a number of resources on business export planning available through the NJBAC Export Promotion unit.
