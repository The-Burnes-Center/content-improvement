Tahesha Way, Secretary of State Chat with us or call us at 1-800-Jersey-7 Resources Ready for You From financial assistance and grants, NJBAC is here for you every step of the way in your business journey. The State and our partners technical assistance and workforce development programs are designed to help you step up your business and maximize your growth potential. If you have any questions or concerns, we’re here to connect you with the right resources to help you succeed. Grant Opportunities Podcasts Publications Webinars Financial Assistance Our advocates can refer you to a variety of financing options available to your business through the NJ Economic Development Authority and community based micro-lending organizations. Technical Assistance Our advocates can assist you with technical business matters like licensing and certifications, business registration and more. Workforce Development We can advise your business on available workforce development services, programs, systems and networks that provide access to skill development for new and existing employees.
