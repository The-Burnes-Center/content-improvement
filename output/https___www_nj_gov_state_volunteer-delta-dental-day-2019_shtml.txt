Tahesha Way, Lt. Governor and Secretary of State Delta Dental All Associate Day Delta Dental of New Jersey hosted the first All Associate Volunteer Day in celebration of their 50th Anniversary. A total of 285 associates contributed 719 hours, working with over 20 community organizations, including the Community Food Bank of NJ, Children’s Specialized Hospital, Eva’s Village, and Habitat for Humanity. Thank you to the wonderful Associates of Delta Dental!
