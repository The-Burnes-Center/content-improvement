Governor and Secretary of State Governor’s Hispanic Fellows Program Accepting 2023 Applications TRENTON, NJ, – As New Jersey celebrates Hispanic Heritage Month (September 15 – October 15), the Center for Hispanic Policy, Research, and Development invites Hispanic New Jersey residents who are enrolled in college to apply for the 2023 class of the Governor’s Hispanic Fellows program. Applications are open through December 1, 2022. The Hispanic Fellows program welcomes 25 students each summer to acquire the skills necessary for success in a professional workplace. For 8 weeks, fellows earn work experience through internships in government, corporate, or non-profit settings while also receiving instruction geared toward leadership and professional development. To learn more about the program or apply to join the 2023 class of Hispanic Fellows, visit https://nj.gov/state/chprd-hispanic-fellows-program.shtml .
