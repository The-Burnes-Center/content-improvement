Governor and Secretary of State Secretary Way Named President-Elect of the National Association of Secretaries of State Des Moines, Iowa – Today, at the 2021 Summer Conference of the National Association of Secretaries of State (NASS), New Jersey Secretary of State Tahesha Way was inducted as the organization’s President-elect. As President-elect, Secretary Way will continue to serve as a member of the NASS Executive Board and is expected to assume the role of NASS President for the 2022-2023 term beginning next July. “I am honored to continue serving my fellow Secretaries of State as a member of the NASS Executive Board and appreciate the trust they are placing in me as this organization’s next President,” said Secretary Way. The full 2021-2022 NASS Executive Board is as follows: President: Secretary Kyle Ardoin, Louisiana President-elect: Secretary Tahesha Way Immediate Past President: Secretary Maggie Toulouse Oliver, New Mexico Treasurer: Secretary Scott Schwab, Kansas Secretary: Secretary Laurel Lee, Florida Eastern Region Vice President: Secretary Jeffrey Bullock, Delaware Southern Region Vice President: Secretary Michael Watson, Mississippi Midwestern Region Vice President: Secretary Steve Simon, Minnesota Western Region Vice President: Secretary Lawerence Denney, Idaho ACR Member-at-Large: Secretary Mac Warner, West Virginia NPA Member-at-Large: Secretary Kimberly Bassett, DC Learn more about NASS and the Executive Board here .
